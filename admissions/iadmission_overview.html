Overview

As you take the smart route to success towards your career, it only makes sense to start with a plan. At South Hills School of Business & Technology, our process begins with a Career Planning Session with our admissions team. Together, we will explore how South Hills can help you to achieve your career goals.

If you would like more information or if you're ready to apply:

  • Fill out the information request form.
  • Our team will follow up with you to schedule a time for you to visit the school to have your personalized Career Planning Session.
  • During the Career Planning Session, you will tour the school, discuss your career, take our entrance assessment, meet with Financial Aid/Faculty/Career Services, and much more. We urge you to bring all of those who will support you during this exciting time (parents, siblings, spouse, loved one, friends, etc.).

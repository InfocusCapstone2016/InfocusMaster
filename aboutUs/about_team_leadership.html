Mark Maggs — Director

Mark Maggs has been the Director of South Hills School of Business & Technology since 2007. He oversees the operations of all three South Hills locations and is the on-site director for the State College main campus.

Mark earned his Bachelor of Science degree in Exercise Science from Lock Haven University. His diverse work experience includes multiple leadership positions in various industries. He has served as Prison Mental Health Therapist, Hospital Orthopedic Rehab Specialist, Subacute Care Director of Therapeutic Recreation, Pharmaceutical and Orthopedic Device Sales, Lumber Industry Human Resources Director, and Furniture Manufacturer Director of Operations.

Mark is passionate about mentoring. He finds it very rewarding to develop our students and help other professionals throughout the community to learn, grow, and become more successful in their own careers.

Outside of work, Mark enjoys many outdoor activities and sports. He also continues his education with an interest in religious studies.

David Schaitkin - Director of Education

David Schaitkin is currently the Associate Director for South Hills School of Business & Technology. In this capacity he assists in the oversight of the three South Hills locations.

David began working at South Hills as a management and marketing instructor and then became the head of the Business Administration: Management & Marketing program position for over ten years. He was then asked to assist in opening a new campus and served as the Co-Director of the Altoona Campus while continuing to teach part-time. After that he moved into the position as the Director of Operations and supervised the operations of all campuses and learning sites. He then moved back to academics as the Director of Education and oversaw curriculum development, compliance with governmental and accrediting agencies, and the hiring, training and supervision of the faculty.

For thirteen years previous to his work in education, David owned and operated his own business. He received his Bachelors degree from Boston University and his M.B.A. from Bloomsburg University in PA. He lives in Lock Haven, PA, has been married for thirty-eight years, and has a daughter living in Pittsburgh. His hobbies include backpacking and cross-country skiing.

Susan Vidmar — Assistant Director of Education

A graduate of Penn State in Speech Communications, Susan Vidmar has been involved in education and academics at South Hills for over 24 years.

Susan has been the Director of Education at South Hills since October 2013. Previous to that, Susan was a Program Coordinator, Internship Coordinator, and Instructor in the Business Administration—Management & Marketing program.

Sandra J. Gibble — Director of Regulatory Affairs

Sandy Gibble has been with South Hills since May 1997.

She’s worked in Academic Affairs and Career Services in addition to being an instructor and Campus Director. She earned a Bachelor of Arts in Industrial/Organizational Psychology from the Pennsylvania State University in 1991 and a Master of Business Administration from the University of Pittsburgh in 1996.

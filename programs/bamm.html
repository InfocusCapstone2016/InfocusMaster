PROGRAMS: BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION - MANAGEMENT & MARKETING

Two-Year Associate in Specialized Business (ASB) Degree Program

The Business Administration—Management & Marketing program at South Hills will provide you with the tools and ability to become an indispensable asset to a company, as well as the leadership and entrepreneurial skills to start your own business. As a member of the BAMM program, you will learn marketing and communication skills, how to create effective advertising and social media campaigns, develop web sites, study selling and purchasing principles, as well as hands-on training in the latest software platforms.

Our BAMM graduates excel in career choices including Sales Manager, Purchasing Agent, Social Media Marketing Executive, Real Estate Agent, Sales & Marketing Specialist, Store or Property Manager and Business Owner/Entrepreneur.

Successful students in this program enjoy collaboration, have good communication skills, are business-minded, success-oriented and enjoy competition.
